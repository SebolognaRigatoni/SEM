The main facilities of Super Earth Mining are dedicated to the managed, democratic process of liberating precious Element-710 fuel from the Terminid swarm. Working alongside tireless helldiver forces, we capture and cultivate these bugs for decomposition, allowing for the extraction of E-710 which powers a diverse set of machinery, from our Super Destroyer fleets to the Super Sedans of the valued citizens on Super Earth.
